Your Takeaway is Here!
Web Design, Speculative Design
School Project
2018.6
My role in this project is web design and taking part in storyline brainstorm.
Many of us eat takeaway food everyday. We are too busy to eat outside with friends or cook a meal by ourselves. What does takeaway food mean to us? Calories? Less social interaction? Working everyday?
We want to explore the relationship between people and takeaway food. And we create an alternative possible future. In 2050, Utopia Takeaway System is created. All people feed on takeaway food sending by drones. The food is custormised according to your taste and you only need one meal to feed your needs everyday. The leading role in this story is Elf, a boy who accidently missed one meal, and then a series of strange things happened.
